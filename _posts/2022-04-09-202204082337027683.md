id="Info.3286"><a href="http://data.eastmoney.com/gdfx/" class="infokey">股东</a></span>南一农集团及其关联方红太阳集团偿还<span id="bk_90.BK0475"><a href="http://quote.eastmoney.com/unify/r/90.BK0475" class="keytip"
