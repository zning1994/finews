<p>住建部国家发改委水利部印发《“十四五”城市排水防涝体系建设行动计划》提出，系统建设城市排水防涝工程体系。评估城市水系蓄水排水能力，优化城市排涝通道及排水管网布局。完善城市河道、湖塘、排洪沟、道路边沟等排涝通道，整治排涝通道瓶颈段。实施防洪提升工程，立足流域全局统筹谋划，依据流域区域防洪规划和城市防洪规划，加快推进河道堤防、护岸等城市防洪工程建设。</p><p><strong>全文如下：</strong></p><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25034883505262226672_w608h862.jpg" width="580" title="941.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25513189162173187033_w609h866.jpg" width="580" title="942.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25364937918049005636_w612h859.jpg" width="580" title="943.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25667406797795049202_w613h865.jpg" width="580" title="944.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D24754946258630996218_w608h865.jpg" width="580" title="945.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25454763833596108907_w611h865.jpg" width="580" title="946.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D24897073798566842851_w612h863.jpg" width="580" title="947.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><p class="em_media">（文章来源：财联社）</p>
