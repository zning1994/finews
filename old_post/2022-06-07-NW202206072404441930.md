<p>据中国钢铁工业协会微信公众号6月7日消息，2022年5月下旬，重点统计钢铁企业共生产粗钢2551.19万吨、生铁2251.28万吨、钢材2492.86万吨。其中，粗钢日产231.93万吨，环比增长0.88%；生铁日产204.66万吨，环比增长0.78%；钢材日产226.62万吨，环比增长2.11%。</p> <center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D24815867047803617351_w835h960.jpg" width="580" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25606924161920946904_w823h50.jpg" width="580" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25716982195669763337_w823h1169.jpg" width="580" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25121849758453679851_w1080h308.jpg" width="580" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25746662523737064180_w1080h942.jpg" width="580" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><p class="em_media">（文章来源：上海证券报）</p>
