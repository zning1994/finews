<p>2022年4月下旬，重点统计钢铁企业共生产粗钢2358.67万吨、生铁2041.27万吨、钢材2293.47万吨。其中，粗钢日产235.87万吨，环比增长5.50%；生铁日产204.13万吨，环比增长4.35%；钢材日产229.35万吨，环比增长6.43%。</p><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D24992248570757595279_w596h639.jpg" alt="图片" width="580" emheight="820" title="1651883224040042435.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25757219615625179934_w582h896.jpg" alt="图片" width="580" emheight="820" title="1651883224040080631.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25571476490062761972_w599h698.jpg" alt="图片" width="580" emheight="820" title="1651883224040081377.png" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" /></center><p class="em_media">（文章来源：中国钢铁工业协会）</p>
