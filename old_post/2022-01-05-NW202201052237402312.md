2。</p><center><img src="https://dfscdn.dfcfw.com/download/D25558905493093291367_w553h311.jpg" alt="图片" style="border:#d1d1d1 1px solid;padding:3px;margin:5px 0;" width="553" /></center><p>在连接技术方面，新的
