 <p>对此，Bloomberg Intelligence的ETF<span id="Info.3224"><a href="http://data.eastmoney.com/invest/invest/default.html" class="infokey">分析师</a></span>Athanasios Psarofagis表示，这是一个相当大的跳跃，当前的元宇宙ETF有PUNK，且Global X和<span web="1" href="http://quote.eastmoney.com/unify/r/105.FUDA" class="em_stock_key_common" data-code="105,FUDA">富达</span>也刚刚提交了申请。所以，元宇宙ETF的竞争将会越来越激烈。</p>
 <p>据悉，METV的持股主要包括软件、互联网和<span id="bk_90.BK1036"><a href="http://quote.eastmoney.com/unify/r/90.BK1036" class="keytip" data-code="90,BK1036">半导体</a></span><span id="bkquote_90.BK1036"></span>类股，包括<span id="stock_105.NVDA"><a href="http://quote.eastmoney.com/unify/r/105.NVDA" class="keytip" data-code="105,NVDA">英伟达</a></span><span id="quote_105.NVDA"></span>(NVDA.US)、<span id="stock_105.MSFT"><a href="http://quote.eastmoney.com/unify/r/105.MSFT" class="keytip" data-code="105,MSFT">微软</a></span><span id="quote_105.MSFT"></span>(MSFT.US)和Meta platform(FB.US)等几乎所有的元宇宙概念热门股。</p>
